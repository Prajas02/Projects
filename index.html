My Todo List

Welcome to My Todo List, a free and open source web app to remember your daily task's . A simple app from us to enhance your productivity.
Steps to get started

Your Task's

    1. Enter Your Task to be done in the input tab.
    2. Click on the button add to List.Your task will be added to your list.
    3. To remove that particular list (whether entered incorrect or completed click on thet task.
    4. Or If you want to empty your whole list than click on the Reset Your list button. Note:(it will reset your list to null and it cannot be brought back).
